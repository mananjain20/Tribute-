About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest cricketers of the modern era and a former captain of the Indian national cricket team. Born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi, India, he is known for his aggressive playing style, exceptional consistency, and strong leadership qualities. Virat Kohli is a right-handed batsman who excels in all three formats of the gameâ€”Test, One Day International (ODI), and T20 cricket. He made his international debut in 2008 and quickly rose to prominence due to his remarkable batting skills and ability to perform under pressure. He has scored thousands of runs across formats and holds numerous records, including being one of the fastest players to reach multiple batting milestones. As a captain, Kohli played a crucial role in transforming the Indian team into a fit, confident, and competitive unit, especially in Test cricket. Under his leadership, India achieved significant victories both at home and overseas. Apart from cricket, Virat Kohli is admired for his discipline, fitness, and dedication. He is also involved in various philanthropic activities and is regarded as a role model for young athletes. Due to his passion, hard work, and achievements, Virat Kohli is often considered an icon of Indian cricket.
